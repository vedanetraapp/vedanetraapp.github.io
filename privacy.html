VEDANETRA — VEDIC ASTROLOGY

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

VedaNetra is built with your privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. Everything stays on your device.

Data Collection

VedaNetra does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All birth information and chart data entered by the user is stored locally on your device only and is never shared with third parties.

We have no access to any data you enter in the app.

Third-Party Services

VedaNetra does not use any of the following:

  • Analytics or crash reporting services (no Firebase, no Crashlytics)
  • Advertising SDKs or ad networks
  • Third-party tracking or user profiling tools
  • Cloud storage or remote databases
  • Social media integrations

In-App Purchases

VedaNetra offers an optional one-time Pro upgrade (VedaNetra Pro) processed through Google Play Billing. Purchase verification is handled entirely by Google Play's infrastructure.

VedaNetra does not access, process, or store any financial or payment data. All billing is managed by Google Play.

Device Permissions

VedaNetra requests only the permissions necessary to function:

  • Storage — to save chart data locally on your device
  • Internet — solely for Google Play purchase verification

No location, camera, contacts, or microphone access is requested.

Children's Privacy

VedaNetra is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from children. As no data is collected from any user, this applies equally to all age groups.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
